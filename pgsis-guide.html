How to Register in GSIS Online

Step 1: Go to GSIS Member Portal

Visit the official GSIS member services page: https://www.gsis.gov.ph/active-members/.

Step 2: Click Register

Click the “Register” button to start creating your GSIS account.

Step 3: Fill Out Your Details

Provide your personal information including GSIS number, email address, and a secure password.

Step 4: Verify Your Account

Check your email for the verification link and activate your GSIS account.

Step 5: Login to Your Account

Use your email and password to log in and access GSIS member services, loan applications, and benefits.
