About Us

Philippine Government Online Services

Philippine Government Online Services is an independent informational website created to help Filipinos easily access official online services of various Philippine government agencies.

Instead of searching multiple websites, users can find direct links to trusted and official portals such as SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, GSIS, PSA, LTO, and NBI in one place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make government online services easier to find, accessible, and understandable for every Filipino.

Independence & Transparency

This website is not affiliated with any government agency. We do not collect personal data, login credentials, or sensitive information.

How We Help

  • Provide direct links to official government portals
  • Save time by compiling services in one website
  • Offer clear guidance through FAQs
