How to Register and Check Pag-IBIG Online

Step 1: Access Virtual Pag-IBIG

Go to the official Virtual Pag-IBIG website: https://www.pagibigfundservices.com/virtualpagibig/.

Step 2: Register an Account

Click the “Register” button and fill in your personal details, including your Pag-IBIG MID number, email address, and password.

Step 3: Verify Your Account

Check your email for the verification link and activate your account.

Step 4: Login to Your Account

Use your email and password to log in. You can now view your Pag-IBIG contributions, savings, and housing loan status.

Step 5: Explore Services

You can apply for Virtual Pag-IBIG transactions, update your profile, and download your Pag-IBIG statements online.
