How to Register and Check Police Clearance Online

Step-by-Step Guide for Philippine Police Clearance Applicants

Police Clearance allows citizens to prove they have no criminal record. Many local police stations now provide **online registration and booking** for convenience. Follow this guide to complete your application safely.

What is Police Clearance Online?

Online Police Clearance lets you:

  • Register and create an account
  • Book and schedule appointments
  • Pay fees online or in-person at designated banks
  • Check application status

Requirements Before Applying

  • Valid government-issued ID (e.g., Passport, Driver’s License)
  • Recent 2x2 photo (some local police require this)
  • Active email address
  • Personal information (name, birthdate, address)

How to Register for Police Clearance Online

Step 1: Go to the official police clearance registration page (varies by local police station, e.g., PNP Clearance Portal).

Step 2: Click “New Registration” and provide your personal details.

Step 3: Verify your email or mobile number if required.

Step 4: Log in to book an appointment and select a payment method.

How to Check Police Clearance Status

Step 1: Log in to your Police Clearance account on the official portal.

Step 2: Click “Check Status” to see if your application has been processed.

Common Problems and Solutions

  • Forgot login details: Use the “Forgot Password” or contact your local police station.
  • No confirmation email: Check spam folder or try re-registering.
  • Appointment full: Choose another date or location.

Safety Tips

  • Use only the official Police Clearance website
  • Do not share login credentials
  • Log out after each session
  • Ensure device security
