How to Register and Check SSS Online

Step-by-Step Guide for Philippine SSS Members

The Social Security System (SSS) allows members to register, log in, and check their contributions online through the official My.SSS portal. This guide will help you do it safely and easily.

What is SSS Online?

SSS Online is the official platform where members can:

  • View contribution records
  • Check loan balances
  • Apply for benefits
  • Update personal information

Requirements Before Registering

  • Valid SSS number
  • Active email address
  • One of the following:
    • Employer ID
    • UMID / SS number
    • Registered mobile number

How to Register for SSS Online

Step 1: Go to the official registration page:

SSS Registration

Step 2: Choose Member Registration.

Step 3: Fill in your personal details correctly.

Step 4: Check your email and click the confirmation link.

How to Log In and Check Contributions

Step 1: Visit the SSS login page:

SSS Login

Step 2: Enter your User ID and Password.

Step 3: View contributions, loans, and benefits.

Common Problems and Solutions

  • Forgot password: Use the “Forgot Password” option.
  • No email received: Check spam folder.
  • Invalid SSS number: Verify details or visit SSS branch.

Safety Tips

  • Use only the official SSS website
  • Never share your password
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi
  • Always log out after use
