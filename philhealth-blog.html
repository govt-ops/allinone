How to Register and Check PhilHealth Online

Step-by-Step Guide for Philippine PhilHealth Members

PhilHealth Online allows members to register, check membership status, and view contribution records safely through the official portal. Follow these steps to access your PhilHealth account.

What is PhilHealth Online?

PhilHealth Online is the official platform for members to:

  • Check membership status
  • View monthly contributions
  • Update personal information
  • Access official forms and guides

Requirements Before Registering

  • PhilHealth Identification Number (PIN)
  • Valid email address
  • Mobile number (optional, for verification)

How to Register for PhilHealth Online

Step 1: Open the official registration page:

Register / Check Account

Step 2: Click "Create Account" and enter required personal details.

Step 3: Confirm your email address via the verification link.

Step 4: Log in to access your PhilHealth records.

How to Check PhilHealth Contributions

Step 1: Go to the PhilHealth Member Inquiry page:

Check Account

Step 2: Log in using your PIN or registered credentials.

Step 3: Review your contributions, premiums, and other benefits.

Common Problems and Solutions

  • Forgot PIN: Use the "Forgot PIN" link to reset.
  • No email received: Check spam folder or try another email.
  • Membership not found: Verify your PhilHealth number or contact PhilHealth.

Safety Tips

  • Always use the official PhilHealth website
  • Do not share your password
  • Log out after each session
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for logging in
