How to Register and Check NBI Clearance Online

Step-by-Step Guide for Philippine NBI Applicants

The National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) provides an online system for applicants to register, book appointments, and check their clearance status. Follow this guide to complete your NBI clearance application safely.

What is NBI Clearance Online?

NBI Clearance Online allows applicants to:

  • Register and create an online account
  • Book and schedule appointments
  • Pay online or in-person at payment centers
  • Check application status

Requirements Before Applying

  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Active email address
  • Payment method for fees (over-the-counter or online)
  • Personal information (name, birthdate, etc.)

How to Register for NBI Clearance Online

Step 1: Open the official NBI Clearance registration page:

Register / Apply

Step 2: Click “New Registration” and provide your personal details.

Step 3: Set a password and verify your email address.

Step 4: Log in to your account to book your appointment and choose a payment method.

How to Check NBI Clearance Status

Step 1: Visit the NBI Clearance login page:

Login

Step 2: Enter your credentials to access your account.

Step 3: Click on “Check Status” to see the progress of your application.

Common Problems and Solutions

  • Forgot password: Use the “Forgot Password” option.
  • No confirmation email: Check spam folder or re-register.
  • Appointment full: Choose a different date or location.

Safety Tips

  • Use only the official NBI website
  • Do not share your login credentials
  • Log out after every session
  • Make sure your device has antivirus protection
